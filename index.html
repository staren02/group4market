Welcome to the Chitechma Student Marketplace

The easiest way for Chitechma students to buy and sell items on campus. List your products, find great deals, and connect with fellow students — all in one place.

Login to Dashboard Browse Products

How It Works

🔑

1. Login with your Matricule

Use your student matricule number and password to access your personal dashboard. Quick and simple.

🛍️

2. Browse Products

View items listed by other Chitechma students — shoes, laptops, bags, textbooks, and much more.

📦

3. Sell Your Items

Have something to sell? Fill in a simple form, set your price, and let other students find your item.

🤝

4. Contact & Deal

Reach out to the seller directly using their contact info. Meet on campus and complete your deal safely.
