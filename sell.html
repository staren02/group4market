Sell an Item

📋 Fill in the details below to list your item on the Chitechma Student Marketplace. Other students will be able to see your listing and contact you.

Product Details

Accepted: JPG, PNG. Max size: 2MB

✅ Tips for a Good Listing

  • Use a clear, specific product name
  • Set a fair and realistic price
  • Be honest about the condition
  • Write a detailed description
  • Add a clear photo if possible
  • Use a phone number buyers can reach

📌 Rules & Guidelines

  • Only Chitechma students can sell
  • No stolen or illegal items
  • Prices must be in FCFA
  • You must be available to be contacted
  • Complete all required fields (*)

❓ Need Help?

If you have a problem with your listing, contact the project team via the Contact Page.
